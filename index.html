Leads the transformation program. Sets the operating cadence, owns the single source of truth, and keeps the work moving end to end.

Later phase

Sponsor

Purpose

Early phase

Later phase

CTO

Sponsor

Purpose

Lead technology advisor. Determines which tools and platforms, and why — ensures the technical foundation supports the program.

Early phase

Supplies negotiated licensing cost data

Later phase

Approves the technology standard per solution type

4

Head of Brand

Independent veto

Purpose

Holds veto over brand risk. Determines what is and isn't acceptable from a brand perspective for any AI initiative.

Early phase

Classifies brand risk by category

Later phase

Sets and signs off on risk thresholds

5

Finance

Value assurance

Purpose

Independent validation of the numbers. Certifies ROI and classifies spend to ensure only solid figures enter the scorecard.

Early phase

Supplies agency-spend data; validates the directional ROI range

Later phase

Certifies net ROI per solution before scorecard entry

6

C&T Lead

Change & capability

Purpose

Manages the change process during ramp-up and coaches an internal successor to take the role over time.

Early phase

Runs comms so original teams stay informed; acts as secretary of the TO weekly

Later phase

Co-leads training as momentum builds, then hands off fully

7

Team Leads

One per brand team

Purpose

The execution layer across all 8 brand teams. Know their team's work, surface the opportunities, and will own what gets built.

Early phase

Identifies and prioritizes each team's AI opportunities

Later phase

Owns delivery of their team's initiatives

Team-level standups run from week one — early on they track diagnostic progress and help shape candidate initiatives; once initiatives are prioritized, the same rhythm shifts to tracking delivery.

Steering committee

Higher-altitude checkpoint. Resolves mandate/scope decisions and unresolved Brand-risk escalations only — all other TO decisions stand without Steering review.

TO weekly action meeting

Fast, fact-based. Default channel for fast-resolvable items is ad-hoc between sessions — the meeting gives visibility to what was resolved and handles what coordination could not close. 60–90 min.

Team standups

Same slot, different job over time: early on it tracks diagnostic progress and helps surface candidate opportunities; later it becomes a day-to-day delivery check for the team's own work.

Meeting details

Day: Last Monday of the month

Duration: 60 min

Frequency: Monthly

Format: In-person preferred

Meeting rules

  • Only Finance-certified figures are presented upward
  • Transformation Lead circulates a one-pager 48 h before
  • Decisions on mandate/scope and unresolved Brand-risk escalations take precedence over TO-level decisions — all other TO decisions stand
  • Each presenter leads their own block — CMO leads value story; COO leads portfolio health
  • Minutes distributed to all TO members within 24 hours

KPIs reviewed

  • Marketing ROI (% return per $ spent)
  • Revenue growth (% YoY)
  • Agency cost savings ($ cumulative)
  • Brand compliance score (0–100)

Meeting details

Day: Monday

Duration: 60 min

Frequency: Weekly

Format: In-person / virtual

Meeting rules

  • Default channel for any fast-resolvable item is ad-hoc (call/chat) between sessions — the meeting reports what was resolved and decides what coordination could not close
  • Finance certifies and classifies spend async before the session; only certified figures carry into the scorecard
  • CMO/CTO priority disagreements are resolved by the TO Lead on the spot — do not escalate to Steering
  • Hard stop at 90 minutes; every agreement logged with owner and deadline

KPIs reviewed

  • Campaign cycle time (days, brief to launch)
  • Content throughput (units produced / time)
  • Cost per asset ($ / unit)
  • Brand review efficiency (% 1st-pass approval)

Meeting details

Days: Tuesday & Thursday

Duration: 15–20 min

Frequency: 2× per week

Format: Standing / virtual

Meeting rules

  • Three questions only: done / doing / blocked
  • No decks, no status reports — spoken updates only
  • Blockers unresolved after 24 h go directly to the TO Lead for ad-hoc resolution (target 48 h) — surface at the TO weekly as information only if still unresolved
  • Team Lead updates the source of truth immediately after

KPIs reviewed

  • Brief to first draft time (hours)
  • Asset first pass approval rate (%)
  • Adoption rate (% of team using tool)
  • Rework rate (% revisions beyond 1st pass)
