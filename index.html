How decisions get made

One right per decision type. Each stakeholder's concern has a structural answer.

Decision Authority What this protects
Initiative prioritization, resourcing, and standards — within delegated budget
 TO Lead (COO)

Against fragmentation. One body decides what moves and in what order. No team can pull in a different direction without TO sign-off.
Technology platform and tool adoption per solution type
 CTO, validated by TO

Against new technical debt. No tool is adopted outside existing enterprise architecture. CTO decides the standard; TO enforces it across all 8 teams.
Brand compliance gate on any AI-generated output before it reaches market
 Head of Brand

Brand standards are not bypassed. Manual review is systematized into a gate — Head of Brand keeps the authority; what changes is the process around it, not the control.
Mandate or scope expansion beyond delegated budget
 CEO at Steering

Board commitment stays credible. No scope creep without CEO sign-off. The CMO's 90-day narrative has a real authority structure behind it.

Steering Committee — monthly session chaired by the CEO. Handles mandate and scope decisions that exceed the TO's delegated budget, and any Brand-risk escalations not closed ad-hoc. All other TO decisions stand without Steering review.

Meeting details

Day: Last Monday of the month

Duration: 60 min

Frequency: Monthly

Format: In-person preferred

Meeting rules

  • Only Finance-certified figures are presented upward
  • Transformation Lead circulates a one-pager 48 h before
  • Decisions on mandate/scope and unresolved Brand-risk escalations take precedence over TO-level decisions — all other TO decisions stand
  • Each presenter leads their own block — CMO leads the business case review; COO leads the initiative progress review
  • Minutes distributed to all TO members within 24 hours

KPIs reviewed

  • Marketing ROI (% return per $ spent)
  • Revenue growth (% YoY)
  • Agency cost savings ($ cumulative)
  • Brand compliance score (0–100)

Meeting details

Day: Monday

Duration: 60 min

Frequency: Weekly

Format: In-person / virtual

Meeting rules

  • Default channel for any fast-resolvable item is ad-hoc (call/chat) between sessions — the meeting reports what was resolved and decides what coordination could not close

  • Content throughput (units produced / time)
  • Cost per asset ($ / unit)
  • Brand review efficiency (% 1st-pass approval)
